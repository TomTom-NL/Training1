Weekly Meal Plan – Tom & Linda

Tom's Menu Linda's Menu Combined Menu Grocery List

Tom's Menu

Day 1: Sunday

Breakfast – Oatmeal with almond milk, chia seeds and sliced apple
Cook 40g oats in 200ml almond milk. Stir in 1 tsp chia seeds and top with 1/2 sliced apple.
Macros: 380 kcal | 15g protein | 45g carbs | 12g fat
Lunch – Grilled salmon with couscous and green beans
Grill 150g salmon with lemon. Serve with 75g cooked couscous and 100g steamed green beans.
Macros: 550 kcal | 45g protein | 30g carbs | 25g fat
Snack – Rice cakes with hummus and cucumber
Spread 2 rice cakes with 2 tbsp hummus and top with sliced cucumber.
Macros: 200 kcal | 6g protein | 20g carbs | 10g fat
Dinner – Chicken stir-fry with rice and mixed vegetables
Stir-fry 150g chicken, 100g veg (carrot, bell pepper, zucchini) in sesame oil. Serve with 70g rice.
Macros: 520 kcal | 45g protein | 45g carbs | 15g fat
Evening Snack – Boiled egg + 5 almonds
Boil 1 egg and have with 5 almonds.
Macros: 100 kcal | 8g protein | 1g carbs | 6g fat

Day 2: Monday

Breakfast – Scrambled eggs with rye toast and carrots
Scramble 2 whole eggs + 1 white. Serve with 1 slice rye bread and raw carrot sticks.
Macros: 350 kcal | 20g protein | 15g carbs | 20g fat
Lunch – Tuna salad with lentils and vinaigrette
Mix 100g tuna, 100g cooked lentils, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and vinaigrette.
Macros: 450 kcal | 35g protein | 30g carbs | 18g fat
Snack – Banana with peanut butter
Slice 1 banana and spread with 1 tbsp peanut butter.
Macros: 220 kcal | 6g protein | 30g carbs | 10g fat
Dinner – Oven-baked cod with sweet potato and broccoli
Bake 150g cod with herbs. Serve with 150g sweet potato and 100g steamed broccoli.
Macros: 500 kcal | 40g protein | 35g carbs | 15g fat
Evening Snack – Coconut yogurt + sunflower seeds
100g coconut yogurt with 1 tsp sunflower seeds.
Macros: 100 kcal | 8g protein | 5g carbs | 5g fat

Day 3: Tuesday

Breakfast – Protein pancakes (oat flour + egg) with berries
Mix 1 egg, 30g oat flour, splash almond milk. Fry and top with 50g berries.
Macros: 370 kcal | 20g protein | 30g carbs | 12g fat
Lunch – Turkey patties with bulgur and roasted vegetables
Pan-fry 2 turkey patties (150g total), serve with 75g bulgur and roasted vegetables.
Macros: 500 kcal | 40g protein | 35g carbs | 18g fat
Snack – Protein shake + rice cake
Shake with plant protein and 1 rice cake.
Macros: 250 kcal | 25g protein | 20g carbs | 5g fat
Dinner – Beef stir-fry with basmati rice and carrots
Stir-fry 150g lean beef with 100g carrots and 70g basmati rice.
Macros: 520 kcal | 45g protein | 40g carbs | 15g fat
Evening Snack – Hard-boiled egg + cherry tomatoes
1 egg with 5 cherry tomatoes.
Macros: 100 kcal | 8g protein | 2g carbs | 5g fat

Day 4: Wednesday

Breakfast – Avocado toast with poached egg
Toast 1 slice rye bread, mash 1/2 avocado on top, and add a poached egg.
Macros: 420 kcal | 20g protein | 25g carbs | 22g fat
Lunch – Grilled chicken wrap with hummus and raw veggies
Wrap 150g grilled chicken in a whole wheat wrap with 1 tbsp hummus and grated carrots.
Macros: 500 kcal | 40g protein | 35g carbs | 18g fat
Snack – Hard-boiled egg + apple
1 egg and 1 small apple.
Macros: 180 kcal | 8g protein | 20g carbs | 6g fat
Dinner – Baked trout with baby potatoes and spinach substitute (e.g. kale)
Bake 150g trout, boil 150g potatoes, steam 100g kale or other leafy green.
Macros: 520 kcal | 45g protein | 35g carbs | 18g fat
Evening Snack – Protein shake + 1 rice cake
1 scoop protein with 200ml almond milk + 1 rice cake.
Macros: 150 kcal | 15g protein | 10g carbs | 3g fat

Day 5: Thursday

Breakfast – Greek-style soy yogurt with berries and oats
Mix 150g dairy-free yogurt with 30g oats and 50g berries.
Macros: 350 kcal | 20g protein | 30g carbs | 10g fat
Lunch – Stuffed bell peppers with minced turkey and quinoa
Stuff 2 peppers with 150g turkey mince and 75g quinoa, bake with tomato sauce.
Macros: 500 kcal | 45g protein | 30g carbs | 15g fat
Snack – Cucumber slices + 2 tbsp hummus
Dip cucumber into hummus.
Macros: 150 kcal | 5g protein | 10g carbs | 10g fat
Dinner – Lamb chops with green beans and sweet potato mash
Grill 150g lamb chops, steam green beans, mash 150g sweet potato.
Macros: 550 kcal | 45g protein | 40g carbs | 20g fat
Evening Snack – 1 boiled egg + 1 tsp olive oil drizzle
Drizzle egg with olive oil.
Macros: 100 kcal | 8g protein | 1g carbs | 6g fat

Day 6: Friday

Breakfast – Omelette with herbs and mushrooms
Cook 3 eggs with mushrooms and fresh herbs.
Macros: 350 kcal | 25g protein | 5g carbs | 22g fat
Lunch – Seared beef slices with spelt and roasted carrots
Pan-sear 150g beef, serve with 75g spelt and roasted carrots.
Macros: 500 kcal | 40g protein | 35g carbs | 18g fat
Snack – Protein bar (no dairy) or 1 rice cake with almond butter
Choose high-protein dairy-free bar or rice cake + 1 tsp almond butter.
Macros: 200 kcal | 20g protein | 15g carbs | 6g fat
Dinner – Salmon poke bowl (cooked salmon) with brown rice and veggies
Serve 150g cooked salmon over 70g brown rice, shredded carrots, cucumber, and soy-lime dressing.
Macros: 500 kcal | 40g protein | 40g carbs | 15g fat
Evening Snack – Coconut yogurt + chia
100g coconut yogurt + 1 tsp chia seeds.
Macros: 100 kcal | 8g protein | 5g carbs | 5g fat

Day 7: Saturday

Breakfast – Muesli with almond milk and pumpkin seeds
Mix 40g muesli with 200ml almond milk and 1 tsp pumpkin seeds.
Macros: 350 kcal | 15g protein | 35g carbs | 12g fat
Lunch – Chicken Caesar salad (no croutons, dairy-free dressing)
Toss grilled chicken, romaine lettuce, and olive-oil lemon dressing.
Macros: 450 kcal | 40g protein | 10g carbs | 20g fat
Snack – 1 boiled egg + 1 slice rye crispbread
Snack on egg and crispbread.
Macros: 150 kcal | 10g protein | 10g carbs | 7g fat
Dinner – Stir-fried shrimp with rice noodles and veggies
Stir-fry 150g shrimp, 70g rice noodles, 100g mixed veg, and soy sauce.
Macros: 520 kcal | 40g protein | 40g carbs | 15g fat
Evening Snack – Berry smoothie with protein powder and water
Blend frozen berries + scoop plant protein + water.
Macros: 100 kcal | 10g protein | 5g carbs | 2g fat
